About

A little about me

I started my career in data and operations — managing databases at a global asset management firm, wrangling IT systems at a manufacturing company. That foundation led me to a small GovTech startup called GovInvest, where I spent five years working my way from IT Analyst to Product Manager Lead. One company, five roles, a lot of learning.

Today I lead product at TrueComp, where our AI platform helps government agencies run compensation studies in weeks instead of months. The work is specific and high-stakes — exactly the kind I like.

On the side, I built Mic Check from scratch: a real-time multiplayer music battle game using the Claude API and Supabase. No team, no playbook. I built it because I wanted to know what I could actually make — and because shipping something you own is the best way to become a better PM.
